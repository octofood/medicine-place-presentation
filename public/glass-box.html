Without Glass Box

  • HC notified weeks after disruption
  • No upstream visibility or leading indicators
  • No product-level tracking by format/strength
  • Emergency tenders: 90-180 days, 300-400% premium

With Glass Box

  • Industry-wide risk signals aggregated daily
  • API supplier, regulatory, price, and shipping tracking
  • Product-level inventory by format, strength, pack size
  • Instant provincial call-ups, ceiling pricing, 48hr delivery
